# worksafe-agent

Long-term research and product-development project on **AI systems for the WorkSafe BC ecosystem** — serving both injured workers managing claims and BC business owners managing claims/appeals.

## Status

Phase: **Discovery / landscape scan** (started 2026-05-14)

## Repo layout

- `research/` — durable findings (domain primer, competitive landscape, regulatory context)
- `opportunities/` — product hypotheses and defensible edges for domain experts
- `log/` — dated session logs (what was done, what was learned, what's next)
- `sources.md` — running bibliography of useful URLs

## Working principles

1. **The domain wins.** Generic legal/claims AI is commoditizing fast (Wisedocs, Spellbook, CanLII Search+). Edge here comes from BC-specific procedural and policy expertise — RSCM, WCAT precedent, presumption clauses — not model quality.
2. **Two distinct user populations.** Worker-side and employer-side have opposite incentives, very different willingness to pay, and different distribution channels. Don't conflate.
3. **The free baseline matters.** Workers' Advisers Office and Employers' Advisers Office both offer free representation. Any paid product must visibly outperform free government help.
4. **Compliance is the lawyer market's moat.** AI-disclosure rules and hallucination sanctions punish generic tools; verifiable, source-grounded retrieval over BC-specific corpora is differentiated.
